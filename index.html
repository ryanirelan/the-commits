Little Git and The Commits

Who Are We?

It all started back in 1992 when three guys met at a college dorm at a university in the middle of Tennessee. Git, the oldest of the bunch, was on his eighth year of college. He had already maxed out fifteen credit cards and students loans to get himself through school. But he was hopelessly lost and confused.

Until he met Tad and Ted.

Tad and Ted, The Commits, were twin brothers and musical prodigies hailing from the roughest part of the upper northwest suburbs of outskirts of Sayerville, NJ. They met Git and started playing. Tad on drums, Ted on bass and Git on guitar.

From that point forward that swore they would play every medium-small venue in every remote college town. And they wouldn't stop until they were done.

And they're still going and going and going and going...

Thank you for checking out our website. See you on the road!
